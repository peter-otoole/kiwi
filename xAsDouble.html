xAsDouble

Usage

xAsDouble(nums)


xAsDouble...(...)

Arguments

nums
a collection of double values. A list, pairlist or vector of length-one double vectors to convert to a double vector.
...
see above.

Value

A double vector.

Description

Convert a collection to a double vector.

Details

xAsDouble converts a list, pairlist or vector of length-one double precision numbers to a double vector. It does not attempt to convert non-double collections to double vectors.

Corner Cases

Integer vectors are coerced to double vectors freely, since the difference is predominantly internal.
